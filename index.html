Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take? +

All domestic orders are delivered within 3–5 business days. International orders may take up to 10 business days depending on your location.

Do you offer international shipping? +

Yes, we ship worldwide. Rates and delivery times vary by destination.

How do I find the right size? +

Each product includes a detailed size chart. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a better fit.

Are your products sustainable? +

Yes, Velisca is committed to sustainability. Our fabrics are ethically sourced, and we follow responsible manufacturing practices.

What is your return policy? +

Items may be returned within 7 days if unworn and in original packaging. Please visit our Return Policy page for full details.
